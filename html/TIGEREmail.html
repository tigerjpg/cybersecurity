These products detonate, analyze, and track suspicious email attachments and embedded URLs and blocks and isolates malicious activity to enhance email security. Thanks to these capabilities, organizations can prevent, detect, and respond to email-based cyber attacks. For additional email security, Tiger also provides anti-spam protection through Email Threat Prevention (ETP) in the cloud. Tiger Email security also provides protection against websites that might use email accounts to promote a product and can result into schemes or spam.

Features

  • Spam detector. This detects any possible spam or source of spam in the email
  • Isolates the threat so it can be tracked and block, so future threats can be prevented
  • Responds to any accidents fast and effectively. It recognizes the source of the problem and neutralizes it.
  • It searches for more threats every 10 minutes throughout the whole system without slowing down the traffic.

Benefits

  • Secure navigation and traffic.
  • Blocks spam, making email accounts less saturated.
  • It gives the security that the data transferred in email will not be compromised.
  • Users can work more efficiently since the environment is threat free.
