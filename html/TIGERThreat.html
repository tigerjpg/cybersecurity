This program detects and fights threats in real time. The programs tells if the data will be attacked or alerts about current attacks. Threat protection also gives the defense team the route of the attack, the IP address, and the location of the attacker. At the end it prints a report of all of the attacks and solutions that occurred in the moment.

Features:

  • Determines threat location (e.g., network vs. endpoint breach) and the extent of the threat in a minimum amount of time.
  • Continuously monitors web/enterprise email, removable media, and web downloads avoiding data losses and breaches. It blocks external sources from seeing or taking any files exchanged within the servers or a single server.
  • Delivers quick comprehensive visibility across multiple communication channels to identify threats. It does not slow or interrupts the flow of data, for the contrary, it deletes any malicious information so data can flow more effectively in between users.
  • Identifies in real-time external and internal threats for rapid mitigation.
  • Delivers superior contextual awareness reducing malware analysis time. It delivers messages across the board with malware threats status.
  • Interoperates with existing mail infrastructures reducing costs and down-time increasing efficiency.

Benefits:

  • Notifies Security and IT of risk protocol / compliance violations made on company endpoints.
  • Provides proprietary analytics and visualizations to enable quick prioritization of alerts.
  • Remediates the highest priority threats in real-time decreasing overall detection and escalation time.
  • It delivers messages to the administrators about any possible, upcoming, or current threats.
  • It emails accounts that at possible risk.
